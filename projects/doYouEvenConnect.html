DoYouEvenConnect


This was an AI I created with a partner that could play Connect Four. It also could extend it to any number of connections (four, five, six, etc). The purpose of this project was to create an AI that could decide on the "best" move within 5 seconds. It does so by predicting all the possible good moves the opponent would make.

In AI, minimax is a very popular choice in terms of search algorithms. For the final iteration of our AI, we chose to implement alpha-beta pruning with minimax. Minimax is the algorithm that minimizes the possible loss for a worst case scenario to maximize the minimum game. In other words, it chooses the best possible move assuming the opponent is looking to maximize their points. Of course, this results in problem of making a move within the time limit. How far in the future can the algorithm go? This was where alpha-beta pruning came in. It allowed us to go farther in the future by removing the moves that can't possibly be better than the other moves. Wikipedia does a really good job of explaining the algorithm.
